Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 14-day free trial for all paid plans. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, GCash, PayMaya, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.

Do you offer discounts for multiple stores?

Yes! We offer volume discounts for customers with multiple store locations. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime. Your account remains active until the end of your billing period, and you can export all your data.

Is my data secure and backed up?

We use enterprise-grade security and perform daily backups. Your data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud.
